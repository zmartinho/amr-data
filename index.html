Global AMR tracks antimicrobial resistance (AMR) around the world. Use the slider to explore resistance rates for different pathogens and antibiotics. The map displays the percentage of tested cultures that were resistant for each country and year. Countries with brighter colors have higher resistance. Gray countries have no available data.
